title: "Connect Gmail, Outlook, Calendar, and Video Conferencing"
sidebarTitle: "Google & Microsoft"
description: "Link your Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 account to Cobalt to send sequences, sync calendar events, and enable automatic meeting transcription."
---

Connecting your email and calendar unlocks the core of Cobalt's outreach and meeting intelligence features. Once linked, you can send and receive emails directly inside Cobalt, let the Balt bot join video calls automatically, and get every meeting transcribed and saved to your candidates' profiles.

<Info>
  Each team member must connect their own account individually. Sequences are sent from each person's own email address.
</Info>

<Warning>
  Meeting transcription requires calendar sync to be active. Without calendar sync, Balt cannot automatically join video calls.
</Warning>

## Connect your account

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Google Workspace">
    Connecting Google Workspace links your **Gmail**, **Google Calendar**, and **Google Meet** to Cobalt.

    ### Capabilities unlocked

    - **Send and receive emails from inside Cobalt** — outreach sequences go out from your Gmail address, and replies land directly in the candidate's Cobalt profile.
    - **Google Calendar sync** — calendar events appear inside Cobalt so your pipeline always reflects your real schedule.
    - **Automatic Balt bot join** — when a Google Meet link is detected on a calendar event, Balt joins the call automatically to transcribe it.
    - **Meeting transcription via Google Meet** — transcripts and summaries are attached to the relevant candidate or contact record.

    ### How to connect

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Integrations">
        In Cobalt, go to **Settings → Integrations → Google Workspace**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Start the connection">
        Click **Sign in with Google**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sign in and grant permissions">
        Sign in with your Google account and authorize access to **Gmail**, **Calendar**, and **Meet** when prompted.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm and start using Cobalt">
        Cobalt confirms the connection. Your sequences can now be sent from your Gmail address, and calendar sync is live.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Microsoft 365">
    Connecting Microsoft 365 links your **Outlook**, **Microsoft Calendar**, and **Microsoft Teams** to Cobalt.

    ### Capabilities unlocked

    - **Send and receive emails from inside Cobalt** — outreach sequences go out from your Outlook address via Microsoft 365, and replies appear directly on the candidate's Cobalt profile.
    - **Microsoft Calendar sync** — calendar events appear inside Cobalt so your pipeline stays in sync with your schedule.
    - **Automatic Balt bot join** — when a Teams meeting link is detected on a calendar event, Balt joins the call automatically to transcribe it.
    - **Meeting transcription via Microsoft Teams** — transcripts and summaries are attached to the relevant candidate or contact record.

    ### How to connect

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Integrations">
        In Cobalt, go to **Settings → Integrations → Microsoft 365**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Start the connection">
        Click **Sign in with Microsoft**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sign in and grant permissions">
        Sign in with your Microsoft account and grant the requested permissions for **Outlook**, **Calendar**, and **Teams**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm and start using Cobalt">
        Cobalt confirms the connection. Your sequences can now be sent from your Outlook address, and calendar sync is live.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
