## General / product

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Cobalt?">
    Cobalt is an all-in-one ATS, CRM, enrichment, automation, and AI platform built specifically for staffing firms (ESN) and recruitment agencies. Instead of juggling a separate ATS, CRM, sourcing tool, and email sequencer, you manage your entire recruitment operation from one place.

    Cobalt was founded in 2025 in Paris by Grégory Hissiger and is designed from day one for the European market.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who is Cobalt for?">
    Cobalt is designed for three main audiences:

    - **ESN (IT services companies)** with consultant teams
    - **Specialist recruitment agencies** — tech, finance, engineering, executive search
    - **Internal HR teams** that use BoondManager and want to add AI-powered sourcing and candidate management

    If you place candidates or manage a consultant bench, Cobalt is built for you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does onboarding take?">
    Most teams are fully operational on day one. All new customers receive a **2-hour onboarding session** with a Cobalt expert — included in all plans.

    Beyond that session, you have access to full documentation in French and English, as well as video tutorials.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free plan?">
    Yes. The **free plan** is available with no time limit. It is a great way to explore Cobalt at your own pace before committing to a paid plan.

    For the full list of included features, see the [Pricing page](https://www.cobalt-ia.com/fr/pricing).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What languages does Cobalt support?">
    The Cobalt interface is available in **French** and **English**.

    Cobalt's AI can read, parse, and process CVs and documents written in French, English, German, Italian, Spanish, Dutch, and Portuguese.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How reliable is the AI?">
    Cobalt's AI relies on semantic search, which finds candidates based on meaning and context — not just exact keyword matching. Precise performance metrics are not published here to avoid unverifiable claims; the team can share concrete evidence during a demo.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is the AI biased?">
    Cobalt takes AI bias seriously and applies several active safeguards:

    1. Regular algorithmic audits [TO CONFIRM: frequency and third-party provider]
    2. Transparent scoring — every AI score shows the specific signals that drove it
    3. Anonymization options — hide names, photos, and other identifiers during the scoring phase
    4. Human final control — Cobalt always recommends; a human always decides

    No candidate is rejected or advanced by AI alone.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Cobalt use my data to train AI models?">
    No. Your data is **never used to train AI models**. This commitment is written into the Data Processing Agreement (DPA) that comes with every plan — making it contractually enforceable, not just a policy.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I migrate from another ATS?">
    Yes. Cobalt offers **free, fully assisted migration** from all major ATS platforms. The migration team handles the data transfer, mapping, and validation — you don't need to prepare anything yourself.

    Supported platforms: Bullhorn, Loxo, Vincere, Recruit CRM, RecruiterFlow, Ezekia, Greenhouse, Workable, Lever, SmartRecruiters, Manatal, JobAdder, Zoho Recruit.

    Not on this list? Contact the team via in-app chat — most platforms can be handled on a case-by-case basis.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Billing & plans

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How is Cobalt priced?">
    Cobalt is priced **per user, per month** across several plans. For the full pricing breakdown and features included per plan, see the [Pricing page](https://www.cobalt-ia.com/fr/pricing).

    Both monthly and annual billing are available.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a discount for annual billing?">
    Yes. Annual billing gives you a discount compared to the monthly rate. You pay 12 months upfront at the lower annual price. You can switch from monthly to annual billing at any time from your account settings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change my plan?">
    Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Plan changes take effect **immediately**. Billing is **prorated** — if you upgrade mid-cycle, you are charged only for the remaining days at the new rate. If you downgrade, the credit is applied to your next invoice. You never lose access to your data during a plan change.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a minimum commitment?">
    No. There is no minimum commitment and no cancellation fee. On a monthly plan, you can cancel at any time; your access ends at the close of the current billing period. On an annual plan, cancelling stops automatic renewal — your access continues until the end of the paid year.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do enrichment credits work?">
    Every paid plan includes a monthly enrichment credit quota shared across your entire team. For quota details by plan, see the [Pricing page](https://www.cobalt-ia.com/fr/pricing).

    When you approach your monthly limit, Cobalt sends you a notification. If you reach the limit, enrichment requests are paused — other features are not affected.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are there add-ons available?">
    Yes. Add-ons are available on paid plans, including VoIP telephony and AI screening. See the [Pricing page](https://www.cobalt-ia.com/fr/pricing) for availability and pricing details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are there implementation or onboarding fees?">
    No. Onboarding is free for all customers. It includes a 2-hour guided session with a Cobalt expert and free migration assistance from your existing ATS or CRM. There are no setup fees.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my data if I cancel?">
    Your data belongs to you. After cancellation:

    - You have **30 days** to export your full database in **JSON or CSV** format
    - Exports are available directly from your account settings — no support request required
    - After 30 days, your data is permanently deleted from Cobalt's systems

    If you need more time to complete your export, contact the team via in-app chat before the 30-day window closes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Security & data

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where is my data hosted?">
    Your data is hosted **100% in Europe**, across two French infrastructure providers:

    - **AWS Paris** — eu-west-3 region
    - **Scaleway** — France

    No data is ever transferred to, processed in, or accessed from outside the European Union. This applies to your candidate records, company data, call recordings, and AI-generated outputs.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is Cobalt GDPR-compliant?">
    Yes — GDPR compliance is built into Cobalt's core workflows, not bolted on as a settings menu.

    Cobalt provides: consent management, right to erasure, data portability (JSON/CSV), breach notification within 72 hours, and a DPA included with every plan.

    For full technical documentation, see the [Security & GDPR](/en/securite/securite-rgpd) page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is data encrypted?">
    Cobalt applies industry-standard encryption at every layer:

    - **At rest**: AES-256 encryption for all stored data
    - **In transit**: TLS 1.3 for all data moving between your browser and Cobalt's servers

    Encryption is always on — it cannot be disabled.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Cobalt use my data to train AI models?">
    No. Cobalt does **not** use your data to train any AI model. This is a **contractual commitment** written into the DPA included with every plan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who owns my data?">
    You own **100% of your data**. Cobalt claims no rights over your candidate records, placement history, company data, or any other information stored in the platform.

    You can export everything at any time from your account settings, in JSON or CSV format.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I delete a candidate from Cobalt?">
    Yes. You can delete any candidate profile in **one click** from the candidate record. Deletion removes the record from your candidate database along with all associated data (enrichment, call recordings, AI summaries). This satisfies the **GDPR right to erasure** (Article 17).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Cobalt have audit logs?">
    Yes. Every action taken in Cobalt — by any user or by an AI agent — is recorded in an **immutable audit log** that is queryable and exportable as CSV. Audit logs are available to all admin users on all plans.
  </Accordion>

    | Penetration tests | [TO CONFIRM] |
    | Bug bounty program | [TO CONFIRM] |

    For more details, see the [Security & GDPR](/en/securite/securite-rgpd) page.

  <Accordion title="Is a DPA available?">
    Yes. A **Data Processing Agreement (DPA)** is included with every Cobalt plan — including the free plan. Enterprise customers can negotiate a custom DPA. To access the standard DPA or request a custom version, contact the team via in-app chat or at [team@cobalt-ia.com](mailto:team@cobalt-ia.com).
