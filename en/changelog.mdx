  ### Cobalt launch

  Cobalt was founded in 2025 in Paris by Grégory Hissiger. The platform is designed for staffing firms and recruitment agencies looking to augment their business development and recruitment activity with AI.

  First features available include:
  - CRM/ATS with semantic search
  - Balt AI agent
  - Multichannel outreach sequences (Email, LinkedIn, WhatsApp)
  - Native BoondManager integration
  - Chrome Extension for LinkedIn profile import
  - Hosted in France, GDPR compliant
</Update>
