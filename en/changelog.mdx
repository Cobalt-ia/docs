<Update label="June 8, 2026" description="Team permissions, multichannel follow-up, project variables, interaction templates">
  ### Team-based permissions

  Permissions now handle **teams**. By assigning a **manager** to each member, you build a **hierarchy** — for example a business unit director above several managers, who in turn are above their business managers.

  A new **Team** access scope lets everyone see **their own records and those of their direct reports**: a business manager only sees their own clients, their manager also sees their team's, and so on.

  <img src="/images/permissions-perimetre.png" alt="Access scopes: All, Own, and Team" width="640" />

  ### Conversation follow-up in projects

  The projects' **Conversations** page becomes **Follow-up**. It lets you **track exchanges** with a project's candidates and prospects, **follow up manually**, and access a **multichannel inbox** (email, LinkedIn, WhatsApp).

  <img src="/images/projet-suivi.png" alt="A project's Follow-up tab, next to Overview and Pipeline" width="320" />

  **Sent emails are now stored** and added to the **Conversations** tabs of candidates and clients, for better follow-up and outreach tracking.

  ### Project variables

  You can now create **custom project variables**, on the fly, without depending on Cobalt's default variables. For example, add a **Title (Mr/Ms)** column: when drafting an email, a new **Project** variable type appears and lets you insert any column, including the ones you just created.

  <img src="/images/variable-projet-picker.png" alt="Variable picker with the Project type (Full name, First name, Last name…)" width="480" />

  Two new default project variables also appear: **First name** and **Last name**, in addition to **Full name** — to insert only the first name, only the last name, or both.

  Adding new variables (new columns) is only possible from the **Excel view**.

  ### Edit a record from a project

  You can now **edit the first and last name** of a candidate or prospect directly from a project's sidebar: click the **three dots** next to the name, then **Edit**.

  <img src="/images/projet-modifier-fiche.png" alt="Record Actions menu in a project: Edit, Delete" width="420" />

  ### Interaction templates

  First version of **interaction templates**: at the end of a meeting, they generate a **summary of the exchange** based on the **meeting type** (candidate, client, qualification, internal). A new interaction is then **created automatically** in the candidate's or client's record.

  <img src="/images/template-interaction.png" alt="Interaction templates: contact and candidate summaries" width="760" />

  <Note>
    An **operational, active workflow** is required for the automatic creation to work.
  </Note>

  ### Email draft preserved

  If you **reload the page** while drafting an email, your **draft is now preserved** — previously, it was erased.

  ### Fixes

  - Fixed the **trailing spaces in emails**, between the end of the message and the signature.
  - Added a **close (×) button on the People Search Engine popup**, for a smoother experience.
  - Fixed **file import in the AI chat**, which was causing errors.
  - Fixed a minor bug.
</Update>

<Update label="June 1, 2026" description="New features and fixes">
  ### Project status and filters

  Projects now have an **Active** or **Archived** status. Archive finalized projects: they no longer appear by default in the list, keeping the projects page focused on what's ongoing. You can also **filter projects by type and by member** — useful in weekly meetings to review everyone's business actions for the week.

  ### MCP documentation in Cobalt

  Cobalt's documentation is now available via **MCP** directly in the app: users can **ask their questions directly to Cobalt**, like a FAQ.

  ### Add clients and companies from a project

  You can now **add new clients (contacts) and new companies directly from a project**, without having to create them first in the associated table.

  <img src="/images/changelog-nouveau-contact.png" alt="Add a new contact from a project" width="520" />

  <img src="/images/changelog-nouvelle-entreprise.png" alt="Add a new company from a project" width="520" />

  ### New workflow block: create a manual task

  A new workflow block lets you **create a manual task** automatically — handy to generate a reminder, for example "call a client".

  <img src="/images/bloc-action-manuelle.png" alt="Workflow block: create a task" width="420" />

  ### Fixes

  - Fixed the **AI chat**.
  - Fixed **meeting transcription**.
  - Fixed **signature creation**.
</Update>

<Update label="May 29, 2026" description="Filters + tasks and transcriptions visibility">
  ### Improved filter management

  You can now **remove an applied filter directly** from the filter bar by clicking the cross on the filter — no need to reopen the menu. A **Reset** button also clears all filters at once.

  <img src="/images/changelog-filtres.png" alt="Filter bar with direct filter removal and a Reset button" />

  ### Tasks and transcriptions visibility

  A **new organization permission** controls the visibility of **tasks** and **transcriptions** (meetings and phone calls): each member can now see only **their own** tasks and transcriptions. Previously, these were visible to everyone by default.
</Update>

<Update label="May 27, 2026" description="LinkedIn Chrome Extension + quality improvements">
  ### LinkedIn Chrome Extension available

  Cobalt's **LinkedIn Chrome Extension** is now available. From a LinkedIn profile, it **enriches the email (work and personal) and phone number**, then lets you add them straight to your **CRM/ATS**, to an **existing project**, or **create a new project** on the fly.

  To install it, go to your **personal settings** and click **Install the Cobalt LinkedIn extension to get started**: the extension installs and syncs with your app.

  <img src="/images/extension-chrome.png" alt="Install the Cobalt LinkedIn extension from settings" />

  ### Quality improvements

  - Improved quality of **enrichment** and **tasks** created from a **meeting** or **phone call** transcription.
  - Improved **People Search Engine** quality.
</Update>
