Cobalt is an AI-powered CRM/ATS built specifically for staffing firms (ESN) and recruitment agencies. It brings together every step of your recruitment workflow — semantic sourcing, contact enrichment, multichannel outreach, meeting transcription, skills document generation, and pipeline tracking — into a single platform. Your built-in AI agent, Balt, handles repetitive tasks so your team can focus on what matters most: relationships and placements.

## Who is Cobalt for?

**Staffing firms (ESN — Entreprises de Services du Numérique)**
You manage a bench of consultants and need to place them quickly on client assignments. Cobalt gives you sourcing speed and branded document generation to win opportunities before your competitors. The native BoondManager sync keeps your operational ERP accurate without double data entry.

**Recruitment agencies**
Whether you run contingency, retained, or RPO mandates, Cobalt centralizes your candidate pipeline, automates repetitive outreach, and enriches contacts — so your recruiters spend their time in conversations, not admin.

**Teams using BoondManager**
If your organization already runs BoondManager, Cobalt connects natively and adds AI-powered sourcing and outreach on top of your existing workflow. Data flows both ways so your records stay consistent.
