WhatsApp is available as an outreach channel within Cobalt's multichannel sequences. You can include WhatsApp steps in your outreach flows alongside Email and LinkedIn steps, to reach candidates on the channel where they are most responsive.

## Use cases

WhatsApp is particularly well-suited for:

- **Conversational follow-ups** — complement an initial email or LinkedIn contact with a more direct and personal message.
- **Hard-to-reach candidates** — some profiles check WhatsApp more regularly than their professional inbox.
- **Quick re-engagement** — a short WhatsApp message can unlock a conversation that has not yet received a response on other channels.

## Integration in sequences

WhatsApp steps are configured directly in Cobalt's sequence builder, in the same way as Email and LinkedIn steps. You choose the channel for each step in the flow and set the delays between sends.

To build and manage your multichannel sequences, see [Sequences](/en/fonctionnalites/sequences).
