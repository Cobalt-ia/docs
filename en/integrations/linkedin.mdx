LinkedIn is the central professional network for recruiters and business developers in staffing firms and recruitment agencies. Cobalt integrates with LinkedIn on two distinct fronts: **profile import** to build your database, and **LinkedIn messages** as an outreach channel within your multichannel sequences.

## Import LinkedIn profiles

LinkedIn profile imports are handled through the **Cobalt Chrome Extension**. When you browse a LinkedIn profile, the extension lets you import it directly into your CRM in one click — no copy-paste required.

<Note>
  Cobalt imports one profile at a time, based on your explicit action. There is no automated or bulk scraping.
</Note>

What is captured during import:

- Contact details (email and phone, automatically enriched after import)
- Work experiences with dates and company names
- Education and certifications
- Skills listed on the profile
- LinkedIn profile URL

To install the extension and learn more about the import process, see [Chrome Extension](/en/integrations/extension-chrome).

## LinkedIn as an outreach channel

Once your LinkedIn account is connected to Cobalt [TO CONFIRM: LinkedIn account connection details], you can include LinkedIn steps in your multichannel outreach sequences:

- **LinkedIn InMail** — to reach candidates who are not part of your network.
- **LinkedIn direct message** — to engage your first-degree connections.

These LinkedIn steps combine with Email and WhatsApp steps in a single sequence flow, with configurable delays between each step.

To build and launch multichannel sequences, see [Sequences](/en/fonctionnalites/sequences).

## Conversation sync

Your **existing LinkedIn conversations** with clients and candidates are synced into Cobalt.

<Note>
  If a person's **LinkedIn link** isn't in Cobalt, their conversation can't be synced. Syncing happens automatically as soon as the LinkedIn link is added.
</Note>

## Points to note

<Warning>
  The exact details of how to connect a LinkedIn account to Cobalt (personal account, company account, LinkedIn restrictions) should be confirmed with your Cobalt contact [TO CONFIRM].
</Warning>

<Info>
  Using LinkedIn in sequences respects LinkedIn's terms of use and limitations. Cobalt does not bypass the platform's native restrictions.
</Info>
