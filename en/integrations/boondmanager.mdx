Cobalt has a native integration with BoondManager. Once connected, candidates, companies, projects, and placements flow automatically between both platforms in real time — so your team works in whichever tool they prefer without ever duplicating data manually.

## What syncs

| Data | Direction |
|---|---|
| Candidates | Cobalt ↔ BoondManager |
| Companies | Cobalt ↔ BoondManager |
| Projects & missions | Cobalt ↔ BoondManager |
| Placements (positionnements) | Cobalt ↔ BoondManager |
| Skills documents | Cobalt → BoondManager |
| Meeting notes & transcripts | Cobalt → BoondManager |

<Info>
  Changes made in BoondManager appear in Cobalt within seconds, and vice versa. No scheduled sync required.
</Info>

## How to connect

<Tip>
  Connect BoondManager before importing other data to avoid duplicate records.
</Tip>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    In Cobalt, navigate to **Settings → Integrations**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select BoondManager">
    Click **BoondManager** in the integrations list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your API key">
    Paste your BoondManager API key. You can find it in BoondManager under **Admin → API**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize the connection">
    Click **Authorize**. Cobalt establishes the connection to your BoondManager account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let the initial sync run">
    The initial sync starts automatically. Cobalt reconciles your existing data across both platforms — no manual intervention required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Cobalt adds on top of BoondManager

BoondManager handles your back-office workflows. Cobalt layers AI and automation on top of the data you already have there:

- **AI-powered semantic search** — find the right candidate from your entire BoondManager base using natural-language queries, not keyword filters.
- **Multichannel outreach sequences** — trigger personalized email and LinkedIn sequences directly from Cobalt using BoondManager candidate and project data.
- **Automatic skills document generation** — Cobalt generates formatted CVs and competency documents and pushes them back to BoondManager profiles.
- **Meeting transcription with auto-update** — every call or video meeting is transcribed and the summary is written back to the matching BoondManager profile automatically.
