Cobalt integrates with the tools you already use — your email, calendar, ERP, and sourcing sources. Each integration is configured from **Settings → Integrations**.

Connecting your tools upfront lets you:

- Send sequences from your own email address and receive replies inside Cobalt.
- Let Balt join your meetings and update your CRM records automatically.
- Sync data with BoondManager without double data entry.
- Source profiles directly from LinkedIn and WhatsApp.

## Available integrations

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="BoondManager" icon="arrows-rotate" href="/en/integrations/boondmanager">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Google Workspace" icon="google" href="/en/integrations/google-workspace">
    Connect Gmail for email sequences and Google Calendar for automatic transcription of your Meet calls.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Microsoft 365" icon="microsoft" href="/en/integrations/microsoft-365">
    Connect Outlook for email sequences and Microsoft Calendar for automatic transcription of your Teams meetings.
  </Card>
  <Card title="LinkedIn" icon="linkedin" href="/en/integrations/linkedin">
    Source and contact LinkedIn profiles directly from Cobalt, without leaving the platform.
  </Card>
  <Card title="WhatsApp" icon="whatsapp" href="/en/integrations/whatsapp">
    Add WhatsApp to your multichannel outreach sequences to reach candidates on their preferred channel.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Chrome extension" icon="chrome" href="/en/integrations/extension-chrome">
    Import profiles from any web page in one click and trigger Cobalt actions directly from your browser.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Each team member must connect their own email and calendar accounts. Connections are not shared between users.
</Note>
