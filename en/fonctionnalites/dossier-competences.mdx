Cobalt generates a complete, professionally formatted skills document — known in French as a *dossier de compétences* (DC) — directly from a candidate's profile, interview notes, and experiences. You control the layout, apply your agency's branding, and send the finished document to your client without ever leaving the platform.

## Generate a skills document

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the candidate profile">
    Navigate to the candidate you want to present and open their profile page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Generate DC'">
    Select **Generate DC** from the profile action menu. Cobalt assembles the document automatically using the candidate's existing data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a template">
    Pick from your saved branded templates — your logo, colours, and fonts are already applied. You can create a dedicated template per client so every document matches their exact visual identity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reorder or hide sections">
    Use the drag-and-drop editor to rearrange sections: Profile, Skills, Experiences, Education, Tools, Languages, and Certifications. Toggle any section off with a single click to hide it from the final document.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview in real time and adjust">
    The live preview updates instantly as you edit, so you always see exactly what the client will receive before you export.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export in your preferred format">
    Download as **PDF**, **Word (.docx)**, or **PowerPoint (.pptx)**, or generate a **shareable online link** you can paste directly into an email or message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite the candidate to review (optional)">
    Send the candidate a customisable email invitation to access their own document portal. They can review and complete their information — you retain final approval before anything goes to the client.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send to the client">
    Click **Send to Client**. Cobalt pre-drafts a covering email aligned to the client's stated need and attaches the DC as a PDF or shareable link. Every send is logged automatically in the CRM for a full history at a glance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Create one template per client with their logo and colour scheme to send perfectly branded documents every time.
</Tip>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does it take to generate a skills document?">
    Generation is near-instant. Cobalt pulls data directly from the candidate's profile, interview notes, and experience history — no manual copy-pasting required.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can the candidate update their own document?">
    Yes. Send them a customisable invitation email; they review and complete their information in a dedicated self-service portal. You approve all changes before the document is sent to the client, so you always retain final control.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What export formats are available?">
    You can export as **PDF**, **Word (.docx)**, **PowerPoint (.pptx)**, or share the document via a **shareable online link** — no download required for your client.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is GDPR anonymisation automatic?">
    Yes. When you select the anonymous export option, Cobalt automatically strips the candidate's name, photo, and personal identifiers from the output. No manual editing is needed.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
