**Document templates** define the layout and visual identity of your documents: logo, colors, fonts, and default template.

Today this mainly covers **skills documents**. Other document types will come in the future — contracts, interview sheets, and more.

You can create multiple templates (per client, per business unit, or per role type) so every generated document is on-brand from the first export.

See [Configure your workspace](/en/demarrer/configurer-espace) to set up document templates.
