## Task recap

Cobalt can **automatically** send you a recap of your tasks — every day or every week, at the **time of your choice**.

To set it up, go to your **personal settings**, **Notifications** section.

<img src="/images/taches-notifications.png" alt="Task recap setting in notifications" width="720" />

Several frequencies are available:

- **Every day** (with a weekly recap on Monday)
- **Every day**
- **Mondays only**
- **Never**

You also choose the **time** the recap is sent.
