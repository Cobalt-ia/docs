Cobalt's enrichment engine fills in the contact details you need to reach candidates without manual research. Whether you are working a single profile or several hundred, Cobalt runs a cascade query across multiple data sources, verifies every email address it finds, and writes the results directly to the candidate's profile.

## Three ways to enrich

<Steps>
  <Step title="Single profile enrichment">
    Open a candidate card and click **Enrich**. Cobalt fetches the verified email address, direct phone number, LinkedIn profile URL, work experience history, listed skills, and education — all in one click. Results appear on the profile within seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bulk enrichment">
    Select multiple profiles from any list view and click **Enrich All**. Cobalt processes profiles in batches and updates each record automatically. Ideal after importing a set of CVs, refreshing a talent pool, or running a new Balt search.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Chrome Extension — enrich directly from LinkedIn">
    Install the [Cobalt Chrome Extension](/en/integrations/extension-chrome). From any LinkedIn profile, click the Cobalt icon to extract the candidate's email and phone number, add them to a Cobalt list, and sync the record to your CRM — all without leaving the LinkedIn tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Cascade method and verification

Cobalt uses a **cascade (waterfall) method**: it queries multiple data providers in sequence and stops as soon as a valid result is found. This multi-source approach maximises coverage while maintaining data freshness.

Email addresses go through a **double verification step** — a syntax check followed by an SMTP check to confirm the mailbox exists and can receive messages. Only addresses that pass both checks are written to the profile.

<Note>
  Effective coverage depends on the profile and sector. Cobalt does not guarantee a fixed coverage rate for every profile type.
</Note>
