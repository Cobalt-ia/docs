You can **share a skills document with the candidate** so they can **review, correct, and complete it**. It's a way to **add process** to recruitment and gather **additional information** about the candidate.

<img src="/images/dossier-partager-candidat.png" alt="Share with the candidate modal: email, instructions, and required fields" width="440" />

## Send the document

From the document, open **Share with the candidate** and fill in:

- The **candidate's email**.
- A **review preset** (default).
- **Instructions for the candidate**.

## Required fields

You can ask the candidate to fill in **required fields** (per experience, general fields, per education…).

- The candidate **cannot validate** their document until the required fields are filled in.
- If they truly cannot answer a field, they must **explain why**.

## Validation

Once the candidate validates the document, the **business manager or HR contact receives an email** confirming that the candidate has validated their skills document.

The document status follows this progression: **Draft** → **Pending** (sent, not yet validated) → **Validated**.
