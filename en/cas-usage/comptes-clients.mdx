Your existing clients are your best growth lever. A client where you have already placed consultants knows your work, trusts your judgment, and has adjacent teams with hiring needs you haven't tapped yet. The challenge is visibility: which teams are expanding, who are the decision-makers you haven't met, and which Business Manager last contacted which contact? Cobalt gives your whole team a live, shared picture of every strategic account — and surfaces the right action at the right moment.

## Why existing accounts are your best opportunity

Your current clients are your most effective growth lever. They have already evaluated your value, their decision cycle is shorter, and every successful mission opens the door to adjacent teams. The challenge is not finding them — they are in your CRM — but activating them in a coordinated and structured way.

## The account growth playbook

<Steps>
  <Step title="Mapping — build a live picture of every account">
    Cobalt builds and maintains a dynamic org chart for each of your client accounts: teams, decision-makers, consultants you've already placed, and contacts who were approached but never converted. Every Business Manager on your team sees the same up-to-date map — never a spreadsheet someone forgot to sync.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Signal detection — know when a need is forming">
    Cobalt monitors public signals continuously: open job postings at client companies, LinkedIn activity from key contacts, funding announcements, press releases, and leadership changes. It correlates those signals with your existing pipeline to surface accounts likely to have a hiring need before they've even briefed you.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Coordinated action — the right message, at the right time">
    Each Business Manager receives targeted, actionable recommendations: which contact to reach out to, on which topic, and with which consultant profile. Pre-drafted proposals and skills documents are ready to attach. Your team acts on intelligence, not instinct — and no two BMs accidentally send conflicting messages to the same contact.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Recurring pipeline — every placement deepens the account">
    When a placement closes, the account map updates automatically: new contacts added, mission feedback recorded, adjacent teams flagged for follow-up. Coverage deepens month over month without any manual upkeep. Every successful mission becomes the foundation for the next one.
  </Step>
</Steps>
