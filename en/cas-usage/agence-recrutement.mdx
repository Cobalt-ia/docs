Running a recruitment agency means juggling multiple open mandates, dozens of active candidates, and clients who all want updates without delay. Most of the friction isn't the work itself — it's the tooling: chasing emails in LinkedIn, reformatting CVs in Word, and finding interview notes buried in a shared Google Doc. Cobalt eliminates that friction so you can focus on what actually drives placements: sourcing the right people, qualifying them well, and presenting them compellingly.

## Before and after Cobalt

| Before Cobalt | With Cobalt |
|---|---|
| Several minutes per profile to track down an email or phone number | Email + phone in 1 click via enrichment |
| Limited LinkedIn approaches per day, modest reply rate | AI-personalized multichannel approaches at scale |
| Interview notes scattered in a Google Doc, impossible to find later | Transcript + AI summary linked directly to the candidate profile |
| Switching between multiple tabs to manage several open mandates | One project per mandate — pipeline, candidates, and client context in one place |
| Manual CV reformatting for each client's preferences | Client-branded skills document generated in seconds |

## How recruitment agencies use Cobalt

<Steps>
  <Step title="New mandate — one project, everything in one place">
    When a client brief lands, create a Project in Cobalt: add the job description, required criteria, salary range, and client context. Every candidate you source, approach, or qualify goes into that project's pipeline. No more cross-referencing spreadsheets or losing a great shortlist in an email thread.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Source without limits">
    Install the Cobalt Chrome Extension and import any LinkedIn profile into your database in a single click. Then enrich it: Cobalt finds a verified professional email and a direct phone number. What used to take several minutes per profile now takes seconds.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Multichannel outreach at scale">
    Set up an automated sequence once: LinkedIn message on day 1, email on day 3, follow-up on day 7. Cobalt's AI personalizes the opening line for each candidate based on their profile — so every approach feels hand-written, even at scale. Your reply rates go up; your admin time goes down.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Qualify without losing context">
    When a candidate interview is done, Cobalt transcribes it and produces a structured summary: motivations, salary expectations, availability, key strengths, and any reservations. That summary links to their candidate profile automatically. When a client asks for feedback on a candidate several weeks later, you have everything — instantly.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Present to client — branded and polished">
    Generate a skills document in one click: the CV is reformatted into the client's template, the qualification summary is included, and the key points are highlighted. Delivered in the client's colors, in seconds. No more reformatting in Word at the last minute before a client deadline.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Cobalt's Chrome Extension works directly inside LinkedIn. You import a profile, enrich it, and add it to a mandate pipeline without leaving your browser tab.
</Note>
