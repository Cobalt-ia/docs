In IT staffing, speed is often the deciding factor. When a client describes a need, the window to win the placement is measured in hours — and every competing firm is calling the same shortlist. Cobalt compresses the entire workflow, from live meeting capture to a branded skills document in the client's inbox, with no manual note-taking, no tedious searching, and no hand-reformatted CVs.

## The IT staffing workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="9:00 — Client meeting: Balt transcribes in real time">
    Your client describes their need — tech stack, seniority level, target daily rate, timeline. Balt, Cobalt's AI agent, joins the meeting and transcribes every exchange in real time. You stay focused on the conversation, not on capturing it.
  </Step>

  <Step title="9:30 — Structured summary saved to the client CRM profile">
    Within minutes of the call ending, Cobalt produces a structured summary: required skills, target daily rate, deadline, and action items. It attaches directly to the client's CRM profile. Zero note-taking, zero risk of missing a detail buried in the conversation.
  </Step>

  <Step title="9:35 — Natural-language search across your entire base">
    Type exactly what your client asked for — *"Java Angular developer, 5+ years of experience, available in 3 weeks"* — and Cobalt searches your entire consultant and candidate base in seconds. No boolean strings, no manual filtering through spreadsheets.
  </Step>

  <Step title="9:40 — Enrich profiles in one click">
    Found the right profiles? Cobalt enriches them instantly: verified professional email, direct phone number, LinkedIn profile — all in a single click. No more hunting across five different tools or calling switchboards.
  </Step>

  <Step title="10:00 — Generate the skills document with the client's branded template">
    Select the candidate and click Generate. Cobalt reformats the CV into a polished skills document using the client's own template — their logo, their colors, their layout. Ready in seconds.
  </Step>

  <Step title="10:15 — Send to client: AI-drafted email, PDF attached">
    Cobalt pre-drafts the client email with the right context from the CRM: the project, the candidate's key strengths, the next step. Attach the PDF, review, send. You are in the client's inbox before your competitors have finished their first search.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The faster you respond, the more likely you are to win the placement. Cobalt keeps you ahead of competing firms by compressing the need-to-skills-document cycle — so you land in the client's inbox first, with a complete and well-formatted proposal.
</Tip>
