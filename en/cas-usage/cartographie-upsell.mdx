Cobalt helps you **map a client account** and **identify the missing contacts** to grow the account (upsell).

## The approach

- **Map the account** — see the teams, decision-makers, and contacts you already know at the company.
- **Identify missing contacts** — spot the decision-makers you haven't approached yet.
- **Trigger upsell** — engage these new contacts through your outreach workflows.

See [People Search Engine](/en/fonctionnalites/people-search) to find the right decision-makers, and [Grow your client accounts](/en/cas-usage/comptes-clients) for the full playbook.
