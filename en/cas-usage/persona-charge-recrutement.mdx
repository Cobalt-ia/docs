For **recruiters**, Cobalt supports day-to-day sourcing:

- **Sourcing on needs** — in support of business managers, on open needs.
- **Continuous sourcing** — on the profiles most likely to interest your current or future clients, based on the sectors the agency focuses on.

See [People Search Engine](/en/fonctionnalites/people-search) and [Source for a need](/en/fonctionnalites/projets/besoin).
