Bench time is one of the most controllable challenges in an IT staffing operation — and one of the most damaging when left unmanaged. Most teams treat bench time reactively: the mission ends, then the scramble starts. Cobalt flips that model. It tracks every mission end date, anticipates upcoming gaps well in advance, and prepares targeted proposals before a consultant ever leaves their current engagement.

## The proactive repositioning playbook

<Steps>
  <Step title="D-60 — The alert before the problem arises">
    Cobalt detects upcoming mission endings and alerts your team well in advance, while the consultant is still on their current engagement. You have time to start proactive outreach to client accounts rather than reacting under time pressure.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Portfolio analysis — surface the best opportunities">
    Cobalt analyses your account portfolio and surfaces the opportunities where the consultant's profile is the strongest fit — based on past missions, tech stack, sector, and client history. No manual searching through spreadsheets.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Proposals ready to send">
    For each identified account, Cobalt prepares a contextualized outreach email and an anonymized, branded skills document. Your Business Manager reviews and approves before anything goes out — nothing is sent without a human in the loop.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Centralized tracking and automated follow-ups">
    Replies are tracked directly in the CRM. For accounts that haven't responded, automated follow-up sequences handle the next touch. Your team focuses on closing, not chasing responses.
  </Step>
</Steps>
