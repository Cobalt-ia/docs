For **business managers / account executives**, Cobalt covers both sides of the job:

- **Prospecting** — find new needs at your clients and prospects, and identify the right decision-makers to contact.
- **Sourcing** — find the right candidates to fill those needs.

See [People Search Engine](/en/fonctionnalites/people-search), [Identify the right decision-makers](/en/fonctionnalites/projets/prospection), and [Source for a need](/en/fonctionnalites/projets/besoin).
