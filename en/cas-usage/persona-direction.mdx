For **executives and managers**, Cobalt provides a steering view of activity — know what's happening without having to ask.

- **Reporting and visibility** — track commercial and recruitment activity across projects, pipelines, and each member's sends/replies.
- **Bench monitoring** — check that actions are actually being taken on consultants who are between assignments.
- **Execution quality** — make sure actions are **prioritized** and **done correctly**, not just "done".

See [Managerial steering](/en/cas-usage/pilotage-activite) and [Projects](/en/fonctionnalites/projets/overview).
