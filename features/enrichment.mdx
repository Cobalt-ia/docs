Cobalt's enrichment engine fills in the contact details you need to reach candidates without manual research. Whether you're working a single profile or thousands, Cobalt runs a cascade query across multiple data providers, verifies every email address it finds, and writes the results directly to the candidate's profile — so your database stays accurate and your team can focus on outreach.

## Three Ways to Enrich

<Steps>
  <Step title="Single profile enrichment">
    Open any candidate card and click **Enrich**. Cobalt fetches the candidate's verified email address, direct phone number, LinkedIn profile URL, work experience history, listed skills, and education — all in one click. Results appear on the profile within seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bulk enrichment">
    Select multiple profiles from any list view and click **Enrich All**. Cobalt processes up to 1,000 profiles within 24 hours and updates each record automatically. Use this after importing a batch of CVs, refreshing a talent pool, or running a new Balt sourcing search.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Chrome Extension — enrich directly from LinkedIn">
    Install the Cobalt Chrome Extension. When you visit any LinkedIn profile, click the Cobalt icon to extract the candidate's email and phone number, add them to a Cobalt list, and sync the record to your CRM — all without leaving the LinkedIn tab. This is the fastest way to build a pipeline while browsing LinkedIn.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Coverage and Data Quality

| Metric | Coverage |
|---|---|
| Email coverage (tech & consultant profiles, France) | 94%+ |
| Phone coverage | 70%+ |
| Average data freshness | < 1 month |

Cobalt uses a **cascade (waterfall) method**: it queries multiple data providers in sequence and stops as soon as it finds a valid result. Email addresses go through a **double verification step** — SMTP check plus syntax validation — before being written to the profile.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="How does Cobalt find contact details?">
  Cobalt runs a cascade (waterfall) query across multiple third-party data providers. Each provider is queried in sequence — the process stops as soon as a valid, verified result is found. This multi-source approach maximises coverage while keeping data fresh and accurate.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is the email data verified?">
  Yes. Every email address goes through a double verification step: a syntax check to confirm the address is correctly formatted, followed by an SMTP check to confirm the mailbox exists and can receive messages. Only addresses that pass both checks are written to the candidate's profile.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I enrich my entire existing database?">
  Yes. Cobalt offers a one-off historical enrichment add-on, available at onboarding. You export your existing database, Cobalt processes it, and returns fully enriched records — no manual work required on your side. Pricing is based on the number of profiles. Speak to your account manager to set this up.
</Accordion>

Credit packs are added to your shared team pool immediately.
</Accordion>
