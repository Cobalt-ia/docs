In IT staffing, speed is the deciding factor. When a client describes a need, the window to win the placement is measured in hours, not days — and every competing ESN firm is calling the same shortlist. Cobalt compresses your entire workflow, from live meeting capture to a branded skills document in the client's inbox, into under 90 minutes. No note-taking, no manual searching, no reformatting CVs by hand.

## The ESN Staffing Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="9:00 — Client meeting: Balt joins and transcribes in real time">
    Your client describes their need — tech stack, seniority, TJM target, timeline. Balt, Cobalt's AI agent, joins the meeting and transcribes every word in real time. You stay focused on the conversation, not on capturing it.
  </Step>

  <Step title="9:30 — Auto-summary saved to the client CRM profile">
    Within minutes of the call ending, Cobalt produces a structured summary: required skills, target TJM, deadline, and action items. It attaches directly to the client's CRM profile. Zero note-taking. Zero risk of missing a detail buried in a wall of text.
  </Step>

  <Step title="9:35 — Natural-language search across your entire base">
    Type exactly what your client asked for — *"Java Angular developer, 5+ years of experience, available in 3 weeks"* — and Cobalt searches your entire consultant and candidate base in under 10 seconds. No boolean strings, no manual filtering through spreadsheets.
  </Step>

  <Step title="9:40 — Enrich profiles in one click">
    Found the right profiles? Cobalt enriches them instantly: verified professional email, direct phone number, and LinkedIn profile — all in a single click. No more hunting across five different tools or calling switchboards.
  </Step>

  <Step title="10:00 — Generate the skills document with the client's branded template">
    Select the candidate and click generate. Cobalt reformats the CV into a polished skills document using the client's own template — their logo, their colors, their layout. Ready in seconds, not 30 minutes.
  </Step>

  <Step title="10:15 — Send to client: AI-drafted email, PDF attached">
    Cobalt pre-drafts the client email with the right context from the CRM: the project, the candidate's key strengths, the next step. Attach the PDF, review, send. You're in the client's inbox before your competitors have finished their first search.
  </Step>
</Steps>
