Cobalt's support team is available Monday to Friday during Paris business hours. Every customer — regardless of plan — has access to the Help Center and documentation. Paid plans include direct access to the support team via in-app chat, with guaranteed response times.

<Tip>
  For fastest resolution, use the **in-app chat**. It opens with your account context already loaded, so the support team can see your plan, integrations, and recent activity and help you immediately — no need to explain your setup from scratch.
</Tip>

## Training & Onboarding

Every new Cobalt customer receives a **2-hour onboarding session** with a Cobalt expert — included in all plans, no extra charge. The session covers your specific use case, team setup, data import, and first workflow configuration.

Beyond the onboarding session, you have access to:

- **Full documentation** in French and English — detailed guides for every feature
- **Video tutorials** in the Help Center — step-by-step walkthroughs for common tasks
- **In-app chat** — for quick questions at any point during your workday

Enterprise customers benefit from an ongoing relationship with a **dedicated Customer Success Manager (CSM)** who proactively monitors your usage, flags opportunities, and helps your team get more value from Cobalt over time.

## Product Roadmap

Cobalt ships **monthly releases** with approximately **2–3 major features per quarter**.

- The **product roadmap is shared quarterly** with all Core, Copilot, and Enterprise customers
- You can **vote on and request features** directly via in-app chat or through your CSM
- Release notes are published in the Help Center after each deployment

If you have a specific feature request or want to share feedback on a workflow, reach out via in-app chat — the product team reads every message.
