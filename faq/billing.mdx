Cobalt's billing is designed to be transparent and flexible — no hidden fees, no lock-in contracts, and no surprise charges. Here are the answers to the most common billing questions. For anything not covered here, reach out via in-app chat.

<Accordion title="How is Cobalt priced?">
  Cobalt is priced **per user, per month**, across four plans:

  | Plan | Price | Billing |
  |---|---|---|
  | Free | €0 | — |
  | Core | €99 / month | Billed annually |
  | Copilot | €249 / month | Billed annually |
  | Enterprise | Custom | Contact us |

  Monthly billing is also available on Core and Copilot, at a 20% higher rate than the annual price.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a discount for annual billing?">
  Yes. Annual billing gives you a **20% discount** compared to the monthly rate. You pay upfront for 12 months at the lower annual price. You can switch from monthly to annual billing at any time from your account settings.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I change my plan?">
  Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Plan changes take effect **immediately**. Billing is **prorated** — if you upgrade mid-cycle, you're charged only for the remaining days at the new rate. If you downgrade, the credit is applied to your next invoice. You never lose access to your data during a plan change.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a minimum commitment?">
  No. There is no minimum commitment and no cancellation fee. On a monthly plan, you can cancel any time and your access ends at the close of the current billing period. On an annual plan, cancelling stops automatic renewal — your access continues until the end of the paid year.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my enrichment quota?">
  When you approach your monthly enrichment credit limit, Cobalt sends you a notification so you're never caught off guard. If you hit the limit, enrichment requests pause — no other features are affected.

  To continue enriching, you can:
  - **Upgrade your plan** to one with a higher monthly quota
  - **Purchase an additional credit pack** (S: 1,000 credits at €49/month, up to XL: 25,000 credits at €499/month)

  There is no automatic overage charge.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do enrichment credits work?">
  Every paid plan includes a monthly enrichment credit quota:

  - **Core**: 500 credits / month
  - **Copilot**: 5,000 credits / month
  - **Enterprise**: Unlimited

  Credits are **shared across your entire team** — all users draw from the same pool. Unused credits **roll over for up to 3 months** before expiring.

  **Credit costs:**
  - 1 professional email address = **2 credits**
  - 1 phone number = **10 credits**
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do add-ons work?">
  Two add-ons are available on all paid plans (Core and above):

  - **VoIP Telephony** — +€20 / user / month: inbound/outbound calls, call recording, automatic transcription, and a dedicated phone number per user
  - **AI Screening** — +€49 / user / month: automated pre-qualification calls, AI scoring, structured call report, and customizable script

  You can activate or deactivate add-ons at any time from your account settings. They are billed per activated user, alongside your main subscription.

  <Note>
    Both add-ons are **included at no extra cost** on the Copilot and Enterprise plans.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Are there implementation or onboarding fees?">
  No. Onboarding is free for all customers. This includes:

  - A **2-hour guided onboarding session** with a Cobalt expert
  - **Free migration assistance** from your existing ATS or CRM
  - Access to the full documentation and video tutorial library

  There are no setup fees, no professional services invoices, and no hidden costs to get started.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens to my data if I cancel?">
  Your data belongs to you. After you cancel:

  - You have **30 days** to export your full database in **JSON or CSV** format
  - Exports are available directly from your account settings — no support ticket required
  - After 30 days, your data is permanently deleted from Cobalt's systems

  If you need more time to complete your export, contact the team via in-app chat before the 30-day window closes.
</Accordion>
