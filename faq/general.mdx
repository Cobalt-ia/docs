Whether you're evaluating Cobalt for the first time or getting started after signing up, this page answers the questions that come up most often. If you don't find what you're looking for here, open the in-app chat — the team responds within 2 hours on business days.

<Accordion title="What is Cobalt?">
  Cobalt is an **all-in-one ATS, CRM, enrichment, automation, and AI platform** built specifically for staffing firms (ESN) and recruitment agencies. Instead of juggling a separate ATS, CRM, sourcing tool, and email sequencer, you manage your entire recruitment operation from one place.

  Cobalt was founded in 2025 in Paris by Grégory Hissiger and is designed from day one for the European market.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Who is Cobalt for?">
  Cobalt is designed for three main audiences:

  - **ESN (Entreprises de Services du Numérique)** with 10 to 500 consultants
  - **Specialist recruitment agencies** — tech, finance, engineering, executive search
  - **Internal HR teams** that use BoondManager and want to add AI-powered sourcing and candidate management

  If you place candidates or manage a consultant bench, Cobalt is built for you.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How long does onboarding take?">
  Most teams are fully operational on day one. Here's what to expect:

  - **Guided setup**: under 1 hour, step by step
  - **Data import**: 10–30 minutes depending on your existing database size
  - **First productive session**: same day you sign up

  All new customers also receive a **2-hour onboarding session** with a Cobalt expert included in their plan.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
  Yes. You get a **14-day free trial** with access to all Core plan features — no credit card required. At the end of the trial, you choose the plan that fits your team, or you can switch to the Free plan if you want to continue at no cost.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a free plan?">
  Yes. The **Free plan** is available with no time limit. It includes:

  - 1 user
  - 3 active projects
  - 5 skills documents per month
  - AI email templates
  - 15–20 semantic searches per month
  - Manual email sending

  It's a great way to explore Cobalt at your own pace before committing to a paid plan.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What languages does Cobalt support?">
  The Cobalt interface is available in **French** and **English**.

  Cobalt's AI can read, parse, and process CVs and documents written in: **French, English, German, Italian, Spanish, Dutch,** and **Portuguese**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How accurate is the AI?">
  Cobalt's AI delivers measurably better results than traditional keyword-based tools:

  - **CV parsing accuracy**: \>95%
  - **Semantic matching quality**: 92% — compared to approximately 65% for boolean keyword search

  Semantic matching finds candidates based on meaning and context, not just exact keyword matches. That means fewer missed candidates and less time spent filtering irrelevant results.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is the AI biased?">
  Cobalt takes AI bias seriously and applies **four active safeguards**:

  1. **Regular algorithmic audits** — independent third-party reviews of AI scoring behavior
  2. **Transparent scoring** — every AI score shows the specific signals that drove it
  3. **Anonymization options** — hide names, photos, and other identifying details during the scoring phase
  4. **Human final control** — Cobalt's AI always recommends; a human always decides

  No candidate is rejected or advanced by AI alone.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Does Cobalt use my data to train AI models?">
  No. Your data is **never used to train AI models** — not OpenAI's, not Anthropic's, and not Cobalt's. This commitment is written into the Data Processing Agreement (DPA) that comes with every plan, so it's contractually enforceable, not just a policy.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I migrate from another ATS?">
  Yes. Cobalt offers **free, fully assisted migration** from all major ATS platforms. The migration team handles the data transfer, mapping, and validation — you don't need to do it yourself.

  **Supported platforms:**
  Bullhorn, Loxo, Vincere, Recruit CRM, RecruiterFlow, Ezekia, Greenhouse, Workable, Lever, SmartRecruiters, Manatal, JobAdder, Zoho Recruit

  **Timeline:** 4–8 weeks depending on database size
  **Data loss:** zero — all records, history, and attachments are migrated

  Not on this list? Contact the team via in-app chat — most platforms can be handled on a case-by-case basis.
</Accordion>
