Cobalt treats data security and GDPR compliance as core product requirements — not afterthoughts. Below are answers to the most common questions about how your data is protected and what rights you have over it. For detailed technical documentation, see the [Security & Compliance](/plans/security-compliance) page.

<Accordion title="Where is my data hosted?">
  Your data is hosted **100% in Europe**, across two French infrastructure providers:

  - **AWS Paris** — eu-west-3 region
  - **Scaleway** — France

  No data is ever transferred to, processed in, or accessed from outside the European Union. This applies to your candidate records, company data, call recordings, and AI-generated outputs.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is Cobalt GDPR-compliant?">
  Yes — and GDPR compliance is built into Cobalt's core workflows, not bolted on as a settings menu.

  Cobalt provides:
  - **Consent management**: track and enforce candidate consent before any contact
  - **Right to erasure**: delete any profile in one click; data is removed from all systems
  - **Data portability**: export any profile or your full database in JSON/CSV at any time
  - **Breach notification**: Cobalt follows GDPR Article 33 — you are notified within 72 hours if a breach affects your data
  - **DPA included**: a Data Processing Agreement is included with all plans, including the Free plan
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How is data encrypted?">
  Cobalt applies industry-standard encryption at every layer:

  - **At rest**: AES-256 encryption for all stored data, including candidate records, documents, and call recordings
  - **In transit**: TLS 1.3 for all data moving between your browser and Cobalt's servers

  Encryption is always on — there is no option to disable it, and no unencrypted data path.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Does Cobalt use my data to train AI models?">
  No. Cobalt does **not** use your data to train any AI model — not OpenAI's, not Anthropic's, and not Cobalt's own models. This is a **contractual commitment** written into the Data Processing Agreement (DPA) that comes with every plan.

  If you are on the Enterprise plan with a custom DPA, this clause is explicitly negotiable and will be included by default.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Who owns my data?">
  You own **100% of your data**. Cobalt claims no rights over your candidate records, placement history, company data, or any other information you store in the platform.

  You can export everything at any time:
  - From your account settings, click **Export** to download your full database
  - Exports are available in **JSON** and **CSV** format
  - No support request is required — the export runs immediately
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I delete a candidate from Cobalt?">
  Yes. You can delete any candidate profile in **one click** from the candidate record. When you delete a profile:

  - The record is removed from your candidate database
  - Associated data is removed from all Cobalt systems, including enrichment data, call recordings, AI summaries, and audit references
  - The deletion is logged in your audit trail for compliance purposes

  This satisfies the **GDPR right to erasure** (Article 17) for candidate deletion requests you receive.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Does Cobalt have audit logs?">
  Yes. Every action taken in Cobalt — by any user or by an AI agent — is recorded in an **immutable audit log**. Audit logs are:

  - **Queryable**: filter by date range, user, action type, or record
  - **Exportable**: download as CSV for compliance reporting or legal review
  - **Immutable**: no user, including admins, can edit or delete log entries

  Audit logs are available to all admin users on all plans.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What certifications does Cobalt have?">
  | Initiative | Status |
  |---|---|
  | ISO 27001 | In progress — expected 2026 |
  | Annual penetration tests | Active |
  | Bug bounty program | Active (responsible disclosure) |

  Cobalt conducts annual penetration tests with an independent external security firm. Results inform the next development cycle. The bug bounty program allows security researchers to report vulnerabilities responsibly.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a DPA available?">
  Yes. A **Data Processing Agreement (DPA)** is included with every Cobalt plan — including the Free plan. You do not need to be a paying customer to access it.

  - **Standard DPA**: available immediately; covers all GDPR requirements for data processors
  - **Custom DPA**: available on the Enterprise plan; negotiable to meet your group's specific legal, contractual, or regulatory requirements

  To access the standard DPA or request a custom version, contact the team via in-app chat or email [team@cobalt-ia.com](mailto:team@cobalt-ia.com).
</Accordion>
