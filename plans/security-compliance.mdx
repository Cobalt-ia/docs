Cobalt is built from the ground up for European recruitment. Your data is hosted exclusively in France, encrypted end to end, and protected by GDPR-native controls you can operate without a legal team. You own 100% of your data — always.

## Infrastructure

**Hosting**
Cobalt runs on **AWS Paris (eu-west-3)** and **Scaleway** (France). Both providers are 100% EU. No data is ever transferred or processed outside the European Union — no exceptions.

**Encryption**
- Data at rest: **AES-256** encryption
- Data in transit: **TLS 1.3**

**Availability**
Cobalt uses a high-availability architecture with automatic failover across availability zones. Infrastructure is monitored 24/7.

## Authentication & Access Control

- **Mandatory MFA** for all admin accounts — you cannot disable this
- **2FA available** for all user accounts (strongly recommended)
- **SSO/SAML** single sign-on is available on the Enterprise plan
- **Granular role-based permissions** — control exactly what each user can see and do, down to individual modules and record types

## GDPR Compliance

Cobalt is GDPR-compliant by design, not by configuration. The controls you need are built into every workflow.

**Consent management**
Candidates must give their explicit consent before you can contact them. Cobalt tracks consent status per candidate and per channel, and surfaces warnings if you attempt to contact an unconsented profile.

**Right to erasure**
Delete any candidate profile in one click. Cobalt removes the record from all systems — including enrichment data, call recordings, and AI-generated summaries — within 30 days.

**Data portability**
Export any individual profile or your entire database at any time in **JSON or CSV** format. No support ticket required.

**Breach notification**
Cobalt maintains incident response processes aligned with **GDPR Article 33**. If a breach affects your data, you are notified within 72 hours.

**Data Processing Agreement (DPA)**
A DPA is included with all plans — you do not need to be on a paid plan to get one. Enterprise customers can negotiate a **custom DPA** tailored to their group's specific requirements.

## AI and Data Use

<Note>
  Your data is **never used to train AI models**. This is a contractual commitment written into the DPA, not just a policy statement.
</Note>

**AI models Cobalt uses:**
- **OpenAI GPT-4.1** — for document generation, email drafting, and summarization
- **Anthropic Claude Sonnet** — for complex reasoning and candidate qualification
- **Proprietary embeddings** — for semantic search across your candidate database

**Human in the loop**
Cobalt's AI proposes — you decide. No autonomous action runs without your explicit validation. AI scoring, matching, and sequence suggestions are always presented as recommendations, not instructions.

## Audit & Transparency

**Audit logs**
Every action taken in Cobalt — by a user or an AI agent — is recorded in an **immutable audit log**. Logs are queryable by date, user, or action type, and exportable in CSV for compliance reporting.

**Explainable AI scoring**
When Cobalt scores a candidate against a role, you can see exactly which skills, experience signals, and criteria drove the score. There are no black-box rankings.

**Anti-bias safeguards**
Cobalt applies four safeguards to reduce AI bias in recruitment:
1. Regular algorithmic audits by an independent third party
2. Transparent scoring — every score shows its reasoning
3. Anonymization options — hide name, photo, and other identifiers during scoring
4. Human final control — a human always makes the final call

## Data Ownership

You own 100% of your data. Cobalt never claims any rights over your candidate records, company data, or proprietary information.

- **One-click export** in JSON or CSV at any time, from your account settings
- **30 days to export** after you cancel your account — your data remains accessible and downloadable
- No vendor lock-in: exported files are structured and portable to any other platform
