## Add-ons

### VoIP Telephony — +€20 / user / month

Add a dedicated phone number and make inbound and outbound calls directly from Cobalt. Every call is automatically recorded and transcribed, so you never miss a detail. Compatible with all paid plans.

### AI Screening — +€49 / user / month

Let Cobalt run automated pre-qualification calls on your behalf. Each call generates an AI score and a structured call report based on your customizable script. Compatible with all paid plans.

<Info>
  AI Screening and VoIP are both **included at no extra cost** on the Copilot and Enterprise plans.
</Info>

### Enrichment Credit Packs

Buy additional enrichment credits on top of your plan's monthly quota. Credits are shared across your entire team and roll over for up to 3 months.

| Pack | Credits / month | Price / month | Price per credit |
|---|---|---|---|
| S | 1,000 | €49 | €0.049 |
| M | 3,000 | €99 | €0.033 |
| L | 8,000 | €199 | €0.025 |
| XL | 25,000 | €499 | €0.020 |

**Credit consumption:**
- 1 professional email address = **2 credits**
- 1 phone number = **10 credits**

### One-off Historical Enrichment

At onboarding, you can enrich your entire existing database in a single batch operation. Pricing is based on total profile volume:

| Volume | Price per profile |
|---|---|
| 0 – 1,000 profiles | €0.15 |
| 1,001 – 3,000 profiles | €0.12 |
| 3,001 – 7,000 profiles | €0.09 |
| 7,001 – 15,000 profiles | €0.07 |
| 15,001 – 30,000 profiles | €0.05 |
| 30,001+ profiles | Custom quote |

Contact the team via in-app chat to get a quote for your historical enrichment.

## Billing FAQ

<Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
  Yes. Every paid plan comes with a **14-day free trial** that includes all Core features. No credit card is required to start. At the end of your trial, you choose the plan that fits your team — or stay on the Free plan.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I change plans?">
  Yes, at any time. Plan changes take effect immediately. Billing is prorated, so you only pay for what you use. You can upgrade or downgrade without losing any of your data.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a minimum commitment?">
  No. You can cancel at any time with no penalty. If you choose annual billing, you benefit from a **20% discount** compared to the monthly rate. Cancelling an annual plan before the end of the year stops renewal — your access continues until the end of the paid period.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my enrichment quota?">
  You receive a notification before you hit your limit. You can immediately upgrade to a larger plan or purchase an additional credit pack. There is no service interruption — your existing data and workflows remain fully accessible.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do add-ons work?">
  VoIP Telephony (+€20/user/month) and AI Screening (+€49/user/month) are optional modules you can activate or deactivate at any time from your account settings. They are compatible with all paid plans (Core and above) and are billed per user, per month alongside your main subscription.
</Accordion>
