Cobalt is an AI-first ATS & CRM built specifically for staffing firms (ESN) and recruitment agencies. It brings together every step of your recruitment workflow — sourcing, candidate enrichment, outreach, interview transcription, skills documents, and placement tracking — into a single platform. Instead of juggling multiple disconnected tools, you work from one place where an AI agent handles the heavy lifting so your team can focus on building relationships and closing placements.

## Who Is Cobalt For?

**ESN (Entreprises de Services du Numérique) with 10–500 consultants**
If you manage a bench of consultants and need to place them quickly on client missions, Cobalt gives you the sourcing speed and branded document generation to win opportunities before competitors do. The BoondManager sync means your operational ERP stays accurate without double entry.

**Recruitment agencies**
Whether you run contingency, retained, or RPO mandates, Cobalt centralizes your candidate pipeline, automates repetitive outreach, and enriches contacts so your recruiters spend time on conversations — not admin.

**Internal HR teams using BoondManager**
If your organization already runs BoondManager, Cobalt plugs in natively and adds AI-powered sourcing and outreach on top of your existing workflow. Data flows both ways so your records stay consistent.
