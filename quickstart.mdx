Cobalt is designed so you can go from sign-up to productive in a single session. This guide walks you through every step — from creating your account to running your first AI-powered candidate search and generating a polished skills document. Most teams complete the full setup in 10 to 30 minutes, and Cobalt's support team is available if you hit any snags along the way.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start Your Free Trial">
    Go to [cobalt-ia.com](https://cobalt-ia.com) and click **Essai gratuit**. Enter your work email address and create a password — no credit card required. Your 14-day free trial starts immediately so you can evaluate the platform with real data before choosing a plan.

    <Note>
      Your trial never auto-charges. At the end of 14 days, you choose whether to upgrade to a paid plan or continue on the Free tier.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Up Your Account">
    After logging in for the first time, Cobalt's guided setup walks you through the basics:

    - **Company name** — used across the platform and in generated skills documents.
    - **Logo** — upload your logo (PNG or SVG) to brand your skills document templates immediately.
    - **Your profile** — add your name, title, and LinkedIn URL so outreach messages come from a real person.

    You can update all of these settings later from **Settings → Workspace**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Your Email">
    Connect your professional email account so Cobalt can send outreach sequences and receive replies on your behalf.

    - **Google Workspace** — click **Connect Gmail** and authorize Cobalt through the standard Google OAuth flow.
    - **Microsoft 365** — click **Connect Outlook** and sign in with your Microsoft account.

    <Info>
      Sequences are sent from your own email address, not a shared Cobalt address. This keeps deliverability high and conversations personal. See [Email & Calendar integrations](/integrations/google-microsoft) for full setup details.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Import Your Candidate Database">
    Bring your existing candidate data into Cobalt so your AI searches start with your internal pool — not a blank slate.

    - **CSV import** — export your current ATS data as a CSV and upload it directly in **Settings → Import**.
    - **ATS migration** — if you're migrating from Bullhorn, Loxo, Vincere, Recruit CRM, or another supported ATS, Cobalt's team handles the migration for you at no extra cost.

    Most imports complete in 10 to 30 minutes depending on database size. Reach out to support if you need help structuring your export file.

    <Tip>
      If you use BoondManager, connect it before importing any data. The bidirectional sync will pull your existing records automatically and prevent duplicates.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run Your First AI Search">
    Open the **Sourcing** tab and type a natural-language request into the Balt search bar. You don't need boolean operators or complex filters — just describe what you're looking for in plain language.

    **Example query:**
    ```text
    Senior Java developer, 5+ years of experience, available within 3 weeks, ideally based in Paris
    ```

    Balt searches your internal database and 800M+ public profiles simultaneously, understands synonyms and job title variants, and returns ranked candidates with match explanations. From the results, you can enrich contact details, add candidates to a project, or launch an outreach sequence in one click.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Your First Skills Document">
    Select any candidate from your results or pipeline and click **Generate Skills Document**.

    1. Choose a template (your default template is pre-configured with your logo and colors).
    2. Review the auto-populated candidate summary — edit any field directly in the editor.
    3. Click **Export** to download a client-ready PDF or Word document.

    The whole process takes about 30 seconds. You can create unlimited skills documents on Core, Copilot, and Enterprise plans.
  </Step>
</Steps>
